Joshua Seth Gordon

My name is Joshua Gordon, I am PhD student in the UCLA Department of Statistics. I completed my M.S. in Statistics at UCLA in 2013 and have since worked as a Data Scientist at Pacific Life Insurance Company in Operations. While originally at UCLA my research focused on Earthquake forecasting using nonparametric modeling techniques.

Prior to attending UCLA I attende Willamette University in Salem, Oregon. After graduation I moved to Portland, Oregon where I lived and worked for a bit over 2 years. While in Portland, I participated in the Americorps VISTA program from 2010 - 2011, organizing financial literacy programs in K-12 schools and to coordinated volunteers for Financial Beginnings , a local 501 (c) 3 non-profit.

Besides my job and research I enjoy working on projects emphasizing web and mobile development. A list of my active projects is below.

When I am not working, you will find me training. Whether it's the gym, cycling along the pacific coast, or helicopter skiing in Canada, I love to push my body and my mind.
